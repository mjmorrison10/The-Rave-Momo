Interactive Artistry

At major music festivals like EDC Las Vegas, Beyond Wonderland, and Countdown, a tall, eerie figure silently weaves through the crowds. Moving with disjointed, slow-motion gestures and staring wide-eyed, they present an striking sight: The Rave Momo.

"NICE TO CREEP YOU!" 👻🫶🏼

This project explores the fascinating intersection of internet horror culture and rave ethos. While the visual aesthetic is drawn directly from the 2018 WhatsApp creepypasta urban legend, the execution is entirely built on PLUR. It acts as a live, highly-engaging performance that challenges the standard rave experience.
